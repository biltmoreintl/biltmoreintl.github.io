About Us

We provide investment banking consulting to smaller, undervalued, rapidly growing companies, undervalued public companies, and undervalued real estate projects. We may be able to introduce you to carefully selected Accredited Investors, Angel investors, Corporate VC's, and traditional VC firms, all fully Accredited. Prefer debt financing with established and regulated financial institutions? We may be able to help you with this, as well. We can also consult on selling and merging your company or project, to generate a large cash payday.

We are not licensed by the SEC or FINRA. We do not sell, transfer, or handle securities; we DO NOT offer investment funds, and we DO NOT provide any investment advice. We bring our experience and resources to the table for the benefit of our rapidly-growing clients, offering private equity, private placements, and syndicated venture capital to accredited investors.
